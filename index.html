Our Mission and Purpose.

773 Concrete Rose is a nonprofit organization founded in 2020 that uses social events to showcase Chicago's talent while simultaneously combating inequality. We use our platform to create positive change around the Chicagoland area. Our mission highlights four main areas: art, education, mentorship, and volunteering. With this project, we hope to expand our brand as one of the most prominent nonprofits in Chicago.
